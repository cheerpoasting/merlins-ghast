Freewill NEW

I used to really believe in free will and that got me through a lot of hard times. My husband always found this idea and obsession kind of odd and would argue against it in favor of "compatibalism" which is just another kind of determinism. But he challenged me a few months back, saying if I really believed in free will I would be able to change some of my personality features, which I know are not changeable. And pondering over that really broke me and I don't believe in free will anymore and its an empty, sad feeling. But I try not to think about it.
