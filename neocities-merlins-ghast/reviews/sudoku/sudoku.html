Sudoku

What is Sudoku?

Sudoku is a pencil puzzle where you try to place the digits 1-9 in every column, row, and box exactly once.

Puzzles vary wildly in difficulty, and during Covid, huge strides were made in Sudoku Theory.

CtC Discord

One of the largest online communities dedicated to solving sudoku and other pencil puzzles is the CtC Discord, named after a YouTube channel "Cracking the Cryptic", which was originally dedicated to solving cryptics crosswords, however evolved into a channel mainly known for solving difficult and variant sudoku puzzles.

Where can I find Sudoku Puzzles?

While there are many places dedicated to sudoku puzzles, the best place to find fresh and challenging puzzles it probably either on the CtC Discord, or on LMD -- though LMD is not a site orginally dedicated to sudoku, but instead was a German puzzle club.
