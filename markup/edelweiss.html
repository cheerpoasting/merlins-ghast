Edelweiss

Edelweiss is a Hungarian psychadelic occult rock band known for their smooth sound and controversial Nazi imagery. Due to the topics covered in their songs, and the shocking artwork on their albums, they are banned from Spotify, though they can be found on YouTube, hosted by Death Fetish. Not much is known about the band members, but rumor has it that the singer only records when he's on psychadelics.

As far as I know, the band does not have any social media, but they can be contacted via email.

Rating: 10.1 -- This is one of my favorite bands, and I love them SO MUCH, but due to the themes and imagery, like talking about the Gestapo and prominent schwastikas on the albums, I can't really share them with anyone. Their sound is so smooth, and their melodies are so haunting, but I always feel scared that if I share my love for this band with other people, people will get the wrong idea about me.

Related Bands: Black Magic SS, behrosth, vampyress, iron butterfly, Assassination
