Mental Health Struggles

I think of this website as a place where I can talk about anything I want. And one of those things that's very important to me is my struggle with mental health. I want to try to find solutions and see patterns, so I'll be blogging about it.

Diagnosis Year Medications Comments
Schizoaffective Disorder 2012 Lamotrogine (since 2016) Sometimes you will see me say I have "depression" or that I have "bipolar disorder". I use these terms for a few reasons, but mainly it is because the conditions are similar and easier for people to understand. When I say these things, referring to myself, I am always referring to Schizoaffective Disorder.
Fibromyalgia Unconfirmed I have never been formally diagnosed with Fibromyalgia, though I do meet all of the criteria. As long as I remain on my medication for Schizoaffective disorder, I have no interest in formal diagnosis, since at this time that is the only medication that works well for the condition and is safe during pregnancy.
Being pale 4000mg Vitamin D Honestly, this has been almost as helpful as my "real" depression medication
Emotional/Social Ignorance I have struggled with understanding social cues and emotional states my whole life, to a degree that has significantly impacted me. When my husband was recently diagnosed with Autism, he says that he was constantly reminded of me while he was answering some of the questions.

Drug Interaction!

I just learned that most cough medicines will interact with my Lamotrogine, making it ineffective, leading to an episode in a similar way to if I hadn't been taking it at all. This is good information for me to know in the future when it comes to handling my episodes.
