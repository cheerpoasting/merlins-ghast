I think documenting the things that I read/study will provide a lot of value to myself, and maybe to you as well.

Otaku Culture Is databased culture debased? Investigating how media has become dominated by tropes.
Otaku Culture The Cult of Character As overarching themes/narratives fall to the wayside, otaku consume media and buy products due to the characters involved. Characters composed of database element (tropes) but lacking in personality perform best, because they can be read into any situation.
Psychiatric Treatment The End of Psychosis Psummer I can really relate to the section where he talks about how people don't understand that Schizophrenics really believe their delusions. My husband's sister is Schizophrenic, and he just can't understand why she believes things that aren't true. I explain to him that that's exactly what makes it a mental illness, but he's a very logical person so it's hard for him to understand.
Japanese Culture Concrete Illusions I think this was an interesting look at the Japanese psychology. I would say though, that at least Japan has good infrastructure, while in the US we live in a crumbling, dangerous mess.
