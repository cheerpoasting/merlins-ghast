They will probably begin their lifecycles here and eventually move elsewhere, as they are better understood. I'm also currently housing my research into how to teach English here as well, and likely it will end up somewhere else in the future.

But, I think that's one of the beauties of having my own website, I can make these kinds of fuzzy decisions, and if I really want, I can change my mind later.
